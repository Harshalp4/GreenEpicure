FAQ

Common Questions

What's the minimum order quantity?

Our MOQs vary by product. We work with businesses of all sizes and can customize quantities to fit your needs. Contact us for specific product minimums.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to 25+ countries worldwide. Our logistics team handles all export documentation and ensures cold chain compliance for temperature-sensitive products.

What certifications do you have?

All products are USDA Organic, NPOP (India), and EU Organic certified. A2 dairy products carry additional A2 protein certification.

How do you ensure quality?

We follow a rigorous 4-step quality process: farm vetting, pre-shipment sampling, third-party lab testing, and arrival verification.
